About Nyakishaba Organic Produce Ltd.

Built for export credibility: farm strength, factory capacity, and nationwide farmer reach.

360-Acre Farm Base

Our farm sits on approximately 360 acres, forming a strong production anchor for consistent supply. We use this base to demonstrate standards, improve seedlings, and support farmer adoption of better practices.

Traceable Sourcing

Farm-to-export control that supports buyer confidence and consistency.

Seedling Support

Improved seedlings and guidance to increase farmer yield and quality.

Factory Development & Value Addition

We are developing a factory to strengthen processing, grading, sorting, and packaging — ensuring consistent export specifications. This value addition improves farmer incomes and helps buyers receive stable quality over long-term supply contracts.

Processing & grading Moisture control Packaging readiness Export documentation support

Machinery sourcing support: HFCC Limited (China) supports our equipment sourcing pipeline and technical inputs.

Operation Wealth Creation Alignment

Our work supports wealth creation through farmer reach, training, and practical support services. Through collaboration with Operation Wealth Creation under Gen. Salim Saleh (Nyakayitana), we aim to strengthen the coffee value chain: from farmer knowledge to post-harvest handling, drying support, and export market access.

Nationwide Farmer Reach

Training & Standards

Good agricultural practices, post-harvest handling, drying, and quality improvement.

Value Addition
