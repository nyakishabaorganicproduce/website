About Nyakishaba Organic Produce Limited

A Ugandan coffee production and export company built on farm strength, value addition, and long-term partnerships.

Who We Are

Nyakishaba Organic Produce Limited is a Ugandan-registered agricultural and export company focused on the production, aggregation, value addition, and export of premium Ugandan coffee. Our operations are built around structured farm control, developing processing capacity, and strong farmer partnerships that support consistent export supply.

Registered in Uganda Export-oriented Farmer-linked supply Value addition focus

360-Acre Farm Base

Our operations are anchored on approximately 360 acres of farmland, which serves as both a production base and a demonstration center for quality standards, traceability, and improved farming practices. This farm base supports consistent supply and provides a model that guides farmer engagement across our wider network.

Traceable Production

Clear sourcing control from farm level through aggregation.

Farmer Support

Improved seedlings, guidance, and post-harvest standards.

Value Addition & Processing

Nyakishaba Organic Produce Limited is developing processing capacity to strengthen grading, moisture control, sorting, and packaging. This approach allows us to meet export specifications consistently while increasing value retained within the supply chain.

Grading & sorting Moisture control Packaging readiness Export compliance

Machinery sourcing and technical inputs are supported through established international supply channels, including HFCC Limited (China).
